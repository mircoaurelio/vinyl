About Us

At Wax Encounters, we believe that every artist deserves the chance to press their music on vinyl—without the barrier of overwhelming upfront costs. Our platform was built to empower musicians, connect them with their fans, and keep the culture of vinyl alive.

How It Works

We use a pre-order system inspired by crowdfunding. Artists can showcase their album with a digital mockup of the vinyl, and fans who are excited about the release can pre-order their copy. Once the funding target is reached, we begin production and ship the finished records directly to supporters.

If the target is not reached within a set number of months, fans will receive a full refund—ensuring that nobody loses money in the process.

This way, artists can bring their music to life without financial risk, and fans become an essential part of the journey.

How It Works

A simple three-step process that connects artists with their fans and brings music to life on vinyl.

1

Artists Upload Their Album

Musicians share their project with us, complete with a digital mockup of the vinyl so fans can see what it will look like.

2

Fans Pre-Order the Vinyl

Supporters place pre-orders to help reach the funding target. If the goal is met, the vinyl goes into production. If not reached within the set time, fans get a full refund—no risk, no loss.

3

We Press & Deliver

Once funded, we handle the printing and ship the finished records directly to the fans who made it possible. Artists release their music without upfront costs, and fans get to spin the vinyl they helped bring to life.
