Our Mission

At Wax Encounters, we believe that every artist deserves the chance to press their music on vinyl—without the barrier of high upfront costs. Our mission is to make vinyl records accessible to independent musicians while giving fans a unique way to support the artists they love.

We've created a system inspired by crowdfunding: artists can showcase their upcoming album with a digital mockup of the vinyl, while fans can pre-order before the record even exists. Once enough pre-orders are reached to cover production, we bring the vinyl to life and notify everyone who backed the project.

This means artists don't need to worry about the heavy initial investment of printing, and fans become an essential part of the creative journey. By pre-ordering, listeners directly help their favorite musicians release music in a tangible form—one that can be collected, cherished, and played for years to come.

Our goal is simple:

Empower artists to release music on vinyl without financial risk.

Connect fans to the creative process, making them collaborators in turning digital music into a physical record.

Celebrate vinyl culture, keeping this timeless format alive for the next generation of listeners.

At Wax Encounters, it's more than just music—it's a community-driven movement where art meets passion, and fans and artists come together to press the future of vinyl.
